Ovum light,product design,2020

蛋是保护的壳，是包含生命的卵。蛋形灯可以作为一件灯具，为人们提供温暖的陪伴。外壳采用毛玻璃制作，内部为白色塑料灯罩和暖黄色LED灯，包含触控装置。

An egg is a protective shell that contains life. The egg-shaped lamp can be used as a lamp to provide warm company for people. The Ovum light is made of frosted glass, with a white plastic lampshade and warm yellow LED lights inside, including touch controls.
